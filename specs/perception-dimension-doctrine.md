## CANONICAL STATEMENT

KRYLO operates in the **perception dimension**, not reality.

Its object is not to reconstruct the world. It is to reconstruct **what each participant
believes the world is** — the latent perceived-state field — from the hard trajectories
they leak (hiring, permits, procurement, shipping, options flow, power demand).

    Reality is the INPUT. Perception is the MEDIUM. The leverage is the DIVERGENCE.

The edge is never any single perceived state being right or wrong. It is the **gap between
perceived fields**, and the fact that some nodes have not yet repriced. Consensus is the
eventual convergence of these fields; leverage exists while the field is still deforming —
i.e. "before it becomes obvious" (§19). Perception is therefore not a new axis; it is the
**spine** the existing subsystems already serve.

## THE LINE THAT KEEPS IT RIGOROUS (load-bearing)

    You do not HOLD perceptions. You RECONSTRUCT them — from grounded observables.

Perception is what KRYLO **emits**; grounded signal is what it **consumes**. Invert that and
the engine collapses into a rumor mill / narrative generator. This single rule is what
separates a perception engine from vibes.

## RELATIONSHIP TO THE TRUTH ENGINE (non-negotiable)

"Reality is irrelevant / there is no variable called Truth" is REJECTED as stated. Truth is
not demoted to irrelevant — it moves from **OUTPUT** to **INPUT INTEGRITY**.

    A Decision Field reconstructed from ungrounded trajectories is a fabrication.
    The pilot wave is only trustworthy because the particle trajectories are real.

Groundedness (§18) and withhold-beats-fabricate (§19) therefore remain fully in force as the
integrity layer on the observables feeding the field. The Truth Engine is the **precondition**
for a trustworthy perceived-state field, not its competitor. A "structurally coherent"
hypothesis with no grounded evidence is a confident fabrication wearing physics — the exact
failure mode the withhold work of 2026-07-09 exists to stop.

## DETECT-NOT-PREDICT SURVIVES INTACT (§11a)

A perceived-state field is a **present fact**: the belief currently producing observable
behavior. KRYLO **detects** the field and its misalignments as they exist now. It does NOT
forecast where consensus will land.

    ALLOWED (detection):  confirmation velocity — how fast each competing hypothesis is being
                          CONFIRMED by evidence as it arrives (retrospective, present-tense).
    FORBIDDEN (prediction): "prediction residual" / "expected next signals" scored as a forecast
                          of tomorrow's observations (forward-tense).

Same math, opposite time-arrow, opposite legality. The frame makes the constraint EASIER to
hold, not harder: leverage = detecting a divergence that already exists, before the network
sees it.

## THE ONE NEW PRIMITIVE (and its guardrails)

competing causal-event interpretations for the same observation cluster is accruing structural
confirmation fastest; surface the **divergence gradient**. Scoring layer on existing engines —
not a new engine.

Hard constraints:

  one latent variable under three names. Audit before they get three weights and triple-count.
